Tagalog Language Challenge!

The word "Tagalog" comes from the combination of two words: "taga," meaning "from," and "ilog," which means "river." The word literally translates to "river dwellers." Tagalog is heavily influenced by several languages, including Spanish, Chinese, Sanskrit, and English.

In this activity, you'll test your Tagalog skills by guessing the English translation of several common, every-day words!
Good luck, and have fun!!!
